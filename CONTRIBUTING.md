# Contributing to Betterdesktopmanager

Thanks to all the amazing people for all your wonderful PRs, issues and ideas, all contributions are welcome!

We are using [dev](https://github.com/risjsnx/betterdesktopmanager/tree/dev) as our development-branch and [main](https://github.com/risjsnx/betterdesktopmanager/main) for the latest stable release. Please provide your pull-requests to the dev branch.

Thank You again for your contribution
