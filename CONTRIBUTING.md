# Contributing to Betterlockscreen

Thanks to all the amazing people for all your wonderful PRs, issues and ideas, all contributions are welcome!

We are using [dev](https://github.com/betterlockscreen/betterlockscreen/tree/dev) as our development-branch and [next](https://github.com/betterlockscreen/betterlockscreen/tree/next) for the latest stable release. Please provide your pull-requests to the dev branch.

For Nix(OS)-Users: We provide a `flake.nix` with devShell for development :-)

Thank You again for your contribution
